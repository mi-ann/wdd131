Site Plan - Creative Arts Blog

Site Name

Creative Arts Hub

This name reflects a space dedicated to exploring various art forms—dance, music, and visual arts—providing a platform for sharing creativity and experiences.

Optional domain availability: creativeartshub.com

Site Purpose

The site will serve as a platform for sharing experiences, tutorials, and tips related to dance, piano, and visual arts, fostering a creative community.

Content Categories

  • Dance Experiences: Share stories about your dance journey and insights learned along the way.
  • Piano Tutorials: Provide guides and videos for beginners learning to play the piano.
  • Art Showcase: Display drawings and artwork, including insights into the creation process.
  • Tips for Aspiring Artists: Offer advice for individuals interested in pursuing dance, music, or visual arts.

Scenarios

  • How can I get started with piano lessons as a beginner?
  • What are some essential tips for improving my dance technique?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #003366 (Used for headings)

Accent Color: #FFA500 (Used for buttons and highlights)

Typography

Font: Roboto (for headings and body text)

Usage: Headings will use Roboto Bold and body text will use Roboto Regular.

Wireframe

Mobile View: Header, main content area for articles, and a footer with contact info.

Desktop View: Grid layout with a sidebar for navigation, larger main content area, and footer with social media links.
